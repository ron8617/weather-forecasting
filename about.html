Frequently Asked Questions

How often is weather data updated?

Our weather data refreshes every 5 minutes for most locations, with severe weather alerts updating in real-time. Premium users get access to 1-minute updates for selected areas.

Is my location data stored?

We only store your location data if you explicitly save favorite locations. Temporary location data used for current weather is deleted after 24 hours. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How does the AI forecasting work?

Our system combines NOAA data with proprietary machine learning models trained on decades of historical weather patterns. The AI identifies subtle atmospheric patterns that traditional models often miss.
